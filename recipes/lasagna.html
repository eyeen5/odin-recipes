Lasagna

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses (which may include ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans whole tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 4 tbsp dried parsley
  • lowfat cottage cheese
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
