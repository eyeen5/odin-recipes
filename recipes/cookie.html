Cookie

A cookie is a baked snack or dessert that is typically small, flat, and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar, egg, and some type of oil, fat, or butter. It may include other ingredients such as raisins, oats, chocolate chips, or nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cupps all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla
  3. Sift in flour and baking soda
  4. Preheat overn to 350 degrees farenheit
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes
